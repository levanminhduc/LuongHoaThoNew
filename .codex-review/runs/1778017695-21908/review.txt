### ISSUE-1: W3 hardcoded-path gate scans endpoint constants
- Category: sequencing
- Severity: critical
- Problem: Step 3.0.5a includes `lib/api/endpoints.ts` in the `/api` hardcoded-path gate, but that file is intentionally supposed to contain `/api/...` endpoint constants.
- Why it matters: The gate will always fail once real endpoint constants are added, blocking W3 even when hooks correctly use `ENDPOINTS`.
- Suggested fix: Remove `lib/api/endpoints.ts` from the hardcoded-path gate. Keep the `VERIFY|Pseudo-paths|TODO|TBD` gate for `endpoints.ts`, and run `/api` hardcoded-path checks only against W3-touched hook/client files.
