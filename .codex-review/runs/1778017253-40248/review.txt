### ISSUE-1: Client fetch audit still excludes some client data layers
- Category: scope
- Severity: high
- Problem: Final audit covers `app components lib/hooks`, but the repo also has direct frontend fetches in `lib/stores/*` and `lib/sync/*`.
- Why it matters: Data fetching can remain outside `apiClient`/React Query while the final audit still passes.
- Suggested fix: Audit all browser-side data roots, at least `lib/stores` and `lib/sync`, or explicitly document and denylist them with proof they are out of scope/server-only.

### ISSUE-2: W5 backlog is not concrete for all admin pages
- Category: scope
- Severity: high
- Problem: The widened audit will surface remaining admin pages like `data-validation`, `column-mapping-config`, `password-reset-history`, `test-column-mapping`, and `employee-check`, but Task 5.9’s table only maps components.

### ISSUE-3: Hook hardcoded-path gate misses backticks
- Category: correctness
- Severity: medium
- Problem: `rg -n "['\"]/api/" lib/hooks/` catches single/double quotes but not template literals like ``apiClient.get(`/api/x`)``.
- Why it matters: A hook can violate the `ENDPOINTS` rule while passing the gate and the fetch audit.
- Suggested fix: Expand the gate to include backticks, e.g. `rg -n "['\"\`]/api/" lib/hooks/`, and handle any existing hits explicitly.
